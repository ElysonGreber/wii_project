Whitepaper Steps

This document details a comprehensive brainstorm on the process of creating, implementing, and launching a new cryptocurrency on the Solana blockchain. The objective is to cover all essential phases, from the initial conception and whitepaper development to launch, marketing, and distribution strategies, including an innovative airdrop mechanism linked to the ownership of specific NFTs.This first phase is crucial and lays the foundation for the entire project. It begins with a clear definition of the cryptocurrency's vision, mission, and objectives. What problem does it solve?

Strategic Planning and Whitepaper

Next, the core team must be formed, defining roles and responsibilities.he whitepaper should cover the introduction to the problem, the solution proposed by the coin, the underlying technology (detailing the use of Solana), the tokenomics model (to be detailed in Phase 2), the development and launch roadmap, the team behind the project, and legal and compliance aspects. The clarity, transparency, and technical and strategic depth of the whitepaper are vital for building credibility and attracting initial interest.
